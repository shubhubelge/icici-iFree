Key Features of iFee+ App

Digital fee payment

The app empowers students to pay their academic fees to their institutes digitally and instantly, anytime from anywhere, through multiple payment modes including UPI, debit/credit card and internet banking platform of any bank.

Other payments

Students can also use the app to send or receive money to/from any UPI ID, or undertake person to merchant (P2M) payments like paying online at merchant sites or paying by scanning QR codes.

Automated reconciliation

Educational institutions can integrate the app with their ERP software which makes their payment reconciliation process automatic and instant. Otherwise, the reconciliation process takes a few business days.

Real time payment tracking

The app allows students to track the real-time status of their fee payment and refunds.

State of the art security framework

The app is backed by an extensive security framework, with firebase authentication and device binding, which ensures the safety of the transactions.

About iFee+

iFee+ offers improved convenience to both educational institutions and students simultaneously. iFee+ empowers students to pay their academic fees to their institutes digitally and instantly. At the same time, it enables institutes to integrate their ERP software with the app, which makes their payment reconciliation process automatic and instant.

Further, the app doubles up as a payments app and offers the facility of person to person (P2P) payments such as sending or receiving money to/from any UPI ID. It also allows person to merchant (P2M) payments like paying online at merchant sites or paying by scanning QR codes. This makes the app a one-stop-solution for students to meet their payment related needs on and off the institution campus.

Students and their parents can register on the app using their institution credentials like roll number and date of birth, check the outstanding fees(tuition, mess and hostel) and make payment instantly through UPI (Unified Payments Interface), debit/credit card or internet banking platform of any bank. They no longer need to stand in long queues to deposit the fee physically through a cheque, DD, challan or cash. Further, the app allows students to track the real-time status of their fee payment and refunds. To begin with, the Bank has tied up with IIT Madras to offer the facility to students of the premier institute. The Bank plans to partner with more such institutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to reset app pin?

Use “Forgot PIN?” option on Verify Pin screen which help you reset PIN using registered mobile number

Unable to see Bank account list during account link?

Please use same mobile number to register to app which is registered with bank , else you will not be able to proceed with any payments .

Who to register to university?

Upon choosing student option from registration, it will be redirected to University page where all the universities available for paying fee will be displayed. Choose the university name and city name accordingly . Student Roll no and DOB need to provide to authenticate .

why am I not able to make payment UPI payment?

Make sure that you have enough balance in your account to make payment, if you still have any concerns cross check your transaction history if the amount is debited or not.

Where to update details of student?

After choosing the university, student registration page appears where all the details i.e. DOB, roll number, email address are to be filled.

How to add bank account?

One the student information is provided then page redirects to bank accounts page, choose add bank account option.

Where to choose the bank accounts?

After bank is chosen, you get list of accounts. Select the account from which fee amount to be deducted.

what is MPIN?

All the UPI payments require to MPIN to make payment securely.

How to make payment?

From student dashboard choose payment due option, it opens payment page

How to change APP pin?

In dashboard of student or parent there is an option to change APP pin.

How to log out of the application?

From Student or Parent dashboard you can use the option logout, it redirects to App verification page.

Where to update any changes in student profile?

In student dashboard / Parent dashboard, choose profile option to update.

How to generate QR code?

Use generate QR code option from dashboard. User can download and share the QR code.

In what ways fee can be payed?

Fee can be paid by 3 ways i.e. using pay now, scan & pay, payment due options.

How to add new bank account?

From Dashboard, use payment settings option to add new bank account.

What is request money?

iFee provides an option to request money by using VPA from which money has to be collected.

How a parent can add one more student details for paying fee?

Use below path to add new student
University Selection >> Student registration >> Student Information >> Add Bank >> Select Bank >> Select Bank Account >> Verify/Store account

How to raise a query related to transaction?

Use the transaction detail page to raise a query.
